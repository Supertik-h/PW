Sport has always been a part of my life, and every form of physical activity brings me energy and joy. Since childhood, I’ve explored various ways to stay active, which has given me a wide range of experiences. For several years, I trained in football, which taught me healthy competition, teamwork, and perseverance. Every match was a lesson in collaboration and striving toward a common goal. At the same time, I developed a passion for long-distance running — it helped me push my own limits and made persistence a core value in my life. Regular gym visits, on the other hand, helped me build strength and improve my physical condition, which empowers me both physically and mentally.

Today, my biggest passion is volleyball. Playing as a middle blocker allows me to make the most of my 182 cm height and constantly develop my skills. I work on my speed, technique, and game awareness to be a reliable support for my team and continuously raise my level on the court. I treat every practice as an opportunity to improve because I believe growth is the key to success. Volleyball gives me the space to fully embrace this passion and strive for new athletic goals.
